about

jim cecconi is an analog photographer based in onekama, michigan. his work focuses on landscapes, naturalism, and architecture. shooting primarily on 35mm film, jim's work is a reflection of his love for the natural world and the beauty found in everday life.

jim's work has been featured in the glasgow gallery of photography's july 2026 'Nature' exhibition.
